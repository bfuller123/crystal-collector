Crystal Collector

A Press Your Luck Game

You are a gem collector, deep in the mines trying to collect enough gems to satiate your boss! Unfortunately, the group of people that built this mine did a poor job of upkeep, and so there is always a chance of it collapsing on you. So do you leave now and upset your boss, or pick until he is content?

Instructions: Click crystals to get as close as you can to the target score, but beware of grabbing too many crystals since it will cause an cave-in!
